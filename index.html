        Game Manual

        This page documents only behavior that is implemented in this build.

        Speed Flags

        Pick a round count, then identify the shown flag from four answers. The clock measures answer speed for bonus points; it does not end the run.

        • Correct score = base country value + combo bonus + fast-answer bonus.
        • Wrong score = at least -280 points or a bigger percentage hit from your current score.
        • Every streak raises the country difficulty pool, so the game becomes less familiar as you keep answering correctly.
        • The selected round count ends the run. Your final score is saved into Runs and Leaderboard.

        Plane Classes

        Fly

        Fly the plane across the country map, manage fuel, and land in the assigned destination country or at a fuel depot. The final score is real math from the route events listed below.

        • Press Begin Takeoff to start moving immediately. Build about 65 m/s, then climb above 70 m to enter cruise.
        • Manual steering works inside the ring. The inner half-area airbrakes while still steering, the outer ring spools acceleration back up, and outside the ring autopilot holds a straight heading.
        • Destination landing points use country-center distance, entry speed, entry altitude, and descent drop over the sampling second.
        • After a destination succeeds, route-completion points are added: 1,200 base + timer bonus + difficulty bonus + fuel bonus + any location bonus.
        • Fuel depots add fuel, points, and tech points based on the same landing quality inputs.
        • Fly targets can now be countries, US state capitals, cities, ancient civilizations, mythic places, and cultural or historical locations shown by a flag or briefing image.
        • Destination targets have no map labels, arrow, or marker. Spend 10 tech points in the pause menu for a 5 second distance scan.

        Fly Tech Tree

        Research points are earned during Fly runs and can be spent during the current session on four upgrade branches.

        FuelLower fuel burn, better route fuel return, stronger depot refuels.
        SpeedHigher thrust and top speed. Speed, acceleration, braking, handling, and upgrades raise the plane class.
        HandlingBetter turn response, stronger banking, cleaner recovery, and higher plane class progress.
        SonarTimed depot pings, destination range help, and ideal landing readouts.
